Christmas Eve

It is not a Silent Night

Today is Christmas Eve. however, i got too many bad news. Walking on the road, i can only feel how the night is bitterly cold.

The academic affairs office is inconsiderate and extremely impatient. but i can’t be rude to them, i must hold onto one firm idea: be able to endure, when possible, stay calm and never easily offend or anger anyone.

Anather thing i realize is the importance of gratitude. When speaking, i must consider whether my words might hurt the people who matter to me. My sisters make strong contribution to my life. Without them, i might have end up as street thug.

If i would to keep a clear mind, i must maintain the midset of ‘ A blessing in disguise’, instead of constantly holding onto a suppressed attitude of complaining about difficulties, which is harmful to my well-being.

Recognizing my current situation, i’m not in the same social class as others.
The 36000 tuition, the 27y March, The 4419.16 scammed

Finally, i quote from Chairman Mao’s words at Huali as an ending: “To defend in order to attack, to retreat in order to move forward, to go sideways in order to move straight, things in this world do not always follow your will. You may want to change direction, but it’s not always possible in an instant.”
